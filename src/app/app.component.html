Knight Academy High School

Welcome to Knight Academy High School! Our mission is to educate our students for their future. From math to history our knightly scholars help students prepare for college or blossom their talents learning skilled trades so each student can have a successful future.

Our chivalrous counselors help each student find the best career path that matches their skills. They also establish backup plans in case a student's initial career plan falls through. In fact, we have numerous electives and clubs that help students hone their skills from computer science and law to programs and activities that teach students valuable trade skills. Knight Academy High School has been around since 1924. In fact, this year marks our 100th year of educating students! Our administration meets with colleges around the city to ensure that our curriculum meets college standards. We also meet with businesses every six months to update our trade programs, so our students are familiar with industry standards. In fact, the students who graduate from our institution have a 75% college acceptance rate over most high schools in the nation!

Here at Knight Academy we have strived to enrich the minds of our students. We offer a unique literature course that has expanded beyond Western authors, to include authors from the East as well. In fact, we asked our fellow alumni what has helped with their career success and most of them stated that reading classic literature and manga improved their working relationships with colleagues from other cultures.

Our math classes use thought-provoking methods and creative teaching styles to help students learn math concepts. This has resulted in our students scoring significantly higher in math and reading proficiency compared to students across the country.
